EDUCATION AT THE DIPLOMA LEVEL

After completing the Sijil Peperiksaan Malaysia (SPM), I was offered the opportunity to further my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Cawangan Kedah. According to the official UiTM website, UiTM Kedah is located in Merbok, Kedah. It was established on October 1, 1997, by YB Tun Daim Zainuddin, the Finance Minister. As shown in the video, it provides a glimpse of the UiTM Kedah campus, with credits to the Majlis Perwakilan Pelajar (MPP).

I am currently enrolled as a student in the Diploma in Information Management (CDIM110) program at the College of Computing, Informatics, and Mathematics. This program is designed to be completed over five semesters. Its primary objective is to address the requirements of information and knowledge workers, who are essential contributors within the information industries. Graduates of this program will acquire a comprehensive set of knowledge and skills, including information literacy, information handling, information communication, technology application management, and various other tasks related to information and knowledge management.
